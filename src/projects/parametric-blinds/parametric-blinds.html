Parametric Blinds

Parametric Blinds with Kinetic Art Elements

The parametric blinds, created as my graduation project, integrate kinetic art elements into a living product, bringing sculptural form and motion to residential, office, commercial, and exhibition spaces. These blinds function similarly to traditional partitions or room dividers that block light, while dynamically transforming spaces with various kinetic animations. Designed to expand the often limited exposure to kinetic art in everyday life, the product allows a broader audience to experience the sculptural beauty of kinetic art.
