DiscoCreative Co., Ltd.: Branding and Logo Variations for a Musicalization Service Company

DiscoCreative Co., Ltd. is a company that offers musicalization services, connecting corporate services with music. As part of a branding renewal project, I designed and proposed materials such as posters and business cards.
Utilizing the strong identity of the word "Disco," I incorporated elements that evoke the image of a disco record.
As part of DiscoCreative’s branding for a multi-purpose cultural space currently in the planning stage, I created logo variations for various services that will be offered at the location. The unfolded shape of a box was interpreted as a geometric form, shaping the word “DiscoBox.” I then transformed and cut parts of the shape to create variations that reflect the different services.
