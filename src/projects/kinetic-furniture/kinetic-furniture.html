Kinetic Furniture

Interactive Furniture with Visual and Auditory Feedback

This piece of furniture amplifies its presence as a centerpiece by producing subtle sounds and movements every time it is opened or closed. The act of interacting with the drawers is enhanced with audiovisual feedback, offering a playful and engaging experience. It was crafted using CNC-milled plywood and square timber, finished with color stain. The top panel’s pieces were created by drilling into leftover plywood, and each piece contains an embedded magnet. These magnets interact with the magnets embedded at the ends of the drawers, creating a repelling force that drives the motion. The small moving elements mimic the ripple of a wave, causing objects placed on the surface to move along with it, adding a sensory layer of visual and auditory enjoyment with every use.
