3D Printed Pen Holder & Desk Clock

This pen holder concept takes inspiration from the undulating layers of paper in wet and dry books. Pens can be inserted between the pages, and the varying widths of the spaces allow for different types of writing tools to find their place. This creates a playful experience of finding the right spot for each pen. The final product was 3D-printed as a single structure.

desk clock is designed using parametric modeling techniques, focusing on gradually changing patterns to form its structure. Depending on the viewpoint, the translucency shifts, creating dynamic visual movement and revealing different aspects of the back. This alters the experience of viewing time based on the clock's placement. The product was 3D-printed as a five-part assembly, designed to fit together without adhesives or fasteners.
